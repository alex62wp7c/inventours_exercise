What is Inventours?

Inventours is a program designed to provide Chief Marketing Officers, Chief Innovation Officers, Heads of R&D, Product Designers, and other Creatives, with actionable insights they can take back to their offices to improve and refresh their own company’s innovation processes and techniques.

Each city program consists of 5 days of carefully curated, exclusive meetings with world-renowned innovators in Product Design, Technology, Food, Sustainability, Fashion, Architecture, and Hospitality. These interactive meetings with highly successful and diverse thought-leaders, are held at their workplaces. Each presenting firm will showcase a range of work, innovation philosophies, process approaches, and diverse stimuli. Expert faculty will lead debrief sessions to make the learning actionable for companies.

How Inventours™ Differs From Other Top-Tier Executive Education Programs



Learning is from highly successful practitioners, not academics, so the program is highly practical, not theoretical


Meetings take place in practitioner workplaces, rather than classrooms, so attendees can observe how different physical environments can impact creativity and collaboration


Inventours™ perspective is global. Attendees will be from different countries, and the programs, by virtue of taking place in global cities known for creativity and design, illustrate how people are working in markets known for innovation


Additional learning comes from interactions with fellow participants. By sharing observations during faculty-led debrief sessions, the learning and observations of the group are magnified through hearing each other’s different perspectives


Sustainable innovation examples are woven throughout the different creative field presentations


Practical application to participants’ own businesses is emphasized.

Barcelona June 9th-14th

Barcelona has long been a hotbed of creativity and independent thinking: home of groundbreaking artists Picasso, Miro and Dali, and the brilliantly original, architect Antoni Gaudi. In cuisine, Ferran Adria is considered the most innovative chef in the world in history. In fashion, Desigual, Custo, and Camper are innovators in color, asymmetry, and style. Buildings such as the gold, fish-shaped Arts Hotel, Jean Nouvel’s Torre Agbar, the colored tile roof of Santa Catarina Market, and the Palace of Music, all have made Barcelona a world-class center of architectural innovation. Barcelona shouts with innovative, original, independent design thinking in all creative fields and so it was selected as the perfect city for an Inventours™ innovation inspiration program.

Copenhagen June 16th-21st

Copenhagen is recognized as “the greenest city in the world”. It has a history of exceptional design, from Georg Jensen silversmiths, Dansk tableware, Royal Copenhagen porcelain, to Scandinavian Design Furniture. Danish cooking, long known for its pastries and baked goods, artistic open faced sandwiches, and rich cheeses and butter, can now boast the #1 ranked restaurant in the world, Noma. This “New Nordic Cuisine” combines centuries old food preparation techniques and locally foraged ingredients with modern takes on presentation. This global design capital was selected for an Inventours™ program because of the breadth and depth of its world class creativity.

New York City July 21st-26th

Considered by many to be the most exciting and dynamic city in the world, perhaps because it has always been a melting pot of cultures, New York thrives on individuality and new ideas. It is a global fashion capital, a gastronomic paradise, a center for art museums and galleries that promote new artistic thinking, and a Mecca for product and packaging design, and cutting edge exterior and interior architecture. In landscape architecture, the High Line elevated park, built on an abandoned railroad track, has captivated the imagination with its unique art installations, outdoor furniture, and native New York plantings. New York provides the ultimate stimulus for creative design and innovation thinking, with its mix of established thought leaders and budding entrepreneurs. Participants will learn and be inspired by the best.

Paris Date TBD

Paris is home to many of the finest pastry and chocolate shops in the world, such as Pierre Hermé, Ladurée, Maison Du Chocolat, and Jean Paul Hevin. The city’s 3-star Michelin restaurants are legendary. Trend-setting retailers such as Colette, and fashion, accessory, and jewelry designers such as Christian Louboutin, Louis Vuitton, Christian Dior, and Van Clef & Arpels, make Paris one of the world’s great centers of style innovation. Architects past and present, such as Gustav Eiffel and Jean Nouvel, and groundbreaking structures such as the Pompidou Center and Quai Branly Museum, make Paris a hotbed of architectural creativity. Color and pattern innovations like Pierre Deux fabrics and toile are constantly being reinvented with different twists. Sensory innovation has also been a hallmark of Paris, as a leading developer of fragrances, with houses such as Givenchy, Chanel and Guerlain. Through Inventours Paris, attendees will experience immersive and stimulating exposure to best-in-class, creative, innovator/practitioners in their workplaces, with real applicability to their own firm’s new product and service development processes.
